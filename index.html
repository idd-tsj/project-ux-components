Why Are People Using Addictive UI?

Money.

  • Advertising
  • Pay per Download
  • Subscriptions
  • Product Sales
  • Service Sales
  • Sponsorships
  • Referral Marketing
  • Selling Data
  • Upsale
  • Transaction Fees
  • Consumable in-app purchases

The more time a user spends on an app = more money for the app's creator. Interfaces are intentionally designed to be addictive in order to acheive specific business goals.

  • Compelled to visit again
  • Compelled to Subscribe
  • Compelled to Buy Something
  • Compelled to Share
  • Compelled to reveal
  • Compelled to stay
